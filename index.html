The Lore

Divided World & Darkness

For 150 years, a man filled with evil intentions — Black Dagger — has lived in secret. Hiding as a friend of the protagonist BSG, he has one ultimate goal: to claim the most dangerous force in existence, REDAGO, which is hidden within BSG’s genes.

But the world itself faces an even greater nightmare. 19 years ago, a being from outer space named DMAGA (known as the God Butcher for killing the strongest beings of the universe) came to Earth. He fought and killed the Strongest Protector of Earth, Rejock. Before leaving, he declared he would return after 20 years and left behind his most powerful weapon, the Godder Sword, as a reminder of the nightmare to come. Now, only one year remains before his return.

Instead of uniting, Earth’s rulers are at war. The West Ruler Hell, locked in conflict with the East Ruler Jiggo Degan, drags the entire planet into global chaos. In this chaos, Hell awakens the Curse God with the help of the Devil Priest Kraven.

Creator Log

Solo Production Pipeline

Creating a full-scale action web series as a solo creator takes tremendous discipline. Here is how Neeraj Saklani crafts Danger X Zone.

Step 1

Scriptwriting & Lore Drafting

Fleshing out the divided directions (North, South, East, West), planning complex faction interactions like the X Government's experiments and plotting major transformations such as the Flame Devil.

Step 2

Art & Model Direction

Designing assets in Season 3's new stylized anime layout. Rigging characters with high expression range, dynamic clothing physics, and stylized textures matching professional animation studios.

Step 3

Animation & Fight Choreography

Using Blender and advanced physics solvers to animate fluid martial arts sequences, teleportation visuals, sword swings, and extreme speed impact animations.

Step 4

Voice Acting & SFX

Recording customized dialog takes and screams for critical battle scenes. Layering complex sound designs consisting of fiery explosions, blade slashes, and heavy aura vibrations.
