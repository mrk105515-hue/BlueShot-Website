Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last Updated: June 13, 2026

This Shipping & Delivery Policy describes our procedures for packaging, dispatching, and delivering pre-ordered merchandise from The BlueShot store.

1. Fulfilling Pre-orders

Please note that all orders placed on our website are **Pre-orders**. Once the pre-order window closes, manufacturing begins. Shirts are printed and packed dynamically based on the pre-order tallies.

  • Production Duration: Manufacturing typically takes 10 to 14 business days after the pre-order campaign finishes.

2. Shipping Timeframes

Once manufacturing is complete, orders are dispatched immediately.

  • India Shipping: Deliveries within India normally take 3 to 5 business days from dispatch.
  • Global Shipping: International shipments can take 7 to 15 business days, depending on location and local customs clearance.

3. Delivery Partners

We work with leading domestic and international courier networks (such as BlueDart, Delhivery, FedEx, and DHL) to ensure secure transit. You will receive an automated email containing your tracking link as soon as your parcel is dispatched from our shipping hub.

4. Contact Us

For tracking assistance, changes to shipping addresses, or delivery inquiries, please reach out to us at blueshotconnectx@gmail.com.
