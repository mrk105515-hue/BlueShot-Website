Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 13, 2026

At The BlueShot, we want to make sure you are completely satisfied with your pre-ordered merchandise. Because our drops are manufactured and shipped based on custom pre-order quantities, please review our policies below regarding cancellations, returns, and refunds.

1. Order Cancellation

You can cancel your pre-order within 24 hours of purchase for a full refund. Once 24 hours have passed, we submit the pre-order tallies to the manufacturer, and order cancellations can no longer be processed.

To request a cancellation, please email us at blueshotconnectx@gmail.com with your Name, Payment ID, and order details.

2. Returns & Replacements

Because all shirts are custom printed based on your selected size, we only accept returns or replacements under the following circumstances:

  • The package or item arrived physically damaged or torn.
  • The print or graphic design has a noticeable production defect.
  • We shipped the incorrect size or incorrect design variation.

To apply for a replacement, contact us within 7 days of delivery with clear photographs showing the damage or mismatch.

3. Refund Processing

Once your return or cancellation is approved, we will initiate a refund. Refunds are credited directly back to the original source account (UPI, Bank Account, or Credit/Debit Card) used during the checkout.

Timeline: Refunds normally take 5 to 7 business days to reflect in your account, depending on your bank's processing cycles.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or would like to request assistance with refunds, please contact us at blueshotconnectx@gmail.com.
