Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 13, 2026

Welcome to The BlueShot ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you visit or make a purchase from our store on www.blueshotwiki.com.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our website or place a pre-order, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, shipping address, and billing address.
  • Order Details: Items purchased, shirt size, quantity, and reference payment IDs.
  • Device & Logging Info: IP address, browser type, and cookies to keep your shopping cart loaded.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following business purposes:

  • To process payments and fulfill pre-orders securely.
  • To email you receipts, order updates, and support messages.
  • To protect our website against fraudulent attempts or unauthorized usage.

3. Payment Processing & Data Security

All payments are securely processed by Razorpay. We do not store or capture your credit/debit card numbers, UPI PINs, or banking passwords on our servers. Razorpay handles your financial credentials in an encrypted secure framework compliant with global security standards.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share transaction details with our payment partner (Razorpay) and fulfillment agents solely to process your orders and ship your packages.

5. Your Rights

If you reside in India or are protected under GDPR or similar regional frameworks, you have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, request corrections, or request deletion of your contact data. Feel free to contact us via the email below to execute these rights.

6. Contact Us

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please email us at blueshotconnectx@gmail.com.
